Teens and School

The high school years are a very significant time in the lives of teens. Most teens are thinking a lot about what the future may hold and what their goals are. Most of the teens in the Survey told us that graduating high school and college were important to them. And most teens reported high levels of school-connectedness.

Teens at Home and in the Community

Teens become increasingly independent and often have busy lives full of school and extracurricular activities. Teens in the Survey told us about participation in a variety of activities. Playing on athletic or sports teams was the most popular activity. Teens' relationships with their parents and siblings are important.

Health and Healthy Behaviors

Most teens are in good health. There are lots of behaviors, however, that can affect a person’s health for better or worse throughout their lives. Researchers wanted to learn about teens’ health and healthy behaviors in order to better understand how behavior in childhood and the transition to adulthood affect life-long health.
